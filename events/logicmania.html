LOGIC MANIA

Abstract

Total No. of Rounds: 3

Round 1: Ddakji (Flip the Card)

Each team member will be given three chances to flip the card. On a successful flip, they will earn 5 points.

Round 2: Quiz

Participants will be given a set of questions covering fun, logical, technical, and basic electrical topics. Each correct answer earns 1 point.

Twist: Participants will receive a punishment for each wrong answer.

Round 3: Logic Gates Costing (Bonus Round)

Each logic gate (e.g., 2-input NAND, 3-input NOR, 1-input inverter) will have an associated cost. Teams will receive a **logic equation** to solve within a given time. Points are awarded based on **cost efficiency**—the team with the **lowest cost solution** will receive **maximum points**.

Registration Fee

  • For 2 members: ₹50
  • For 3 members: ₹80

Winners

The **team with the maximum points** by the end of all three rounds will be declared the **winner**.
