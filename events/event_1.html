CODEFIESTA

The Department of EEE(Electret) presents “CODEFISTA” , a technical event loaded with lots of fun.

Total no. of rounds: 3

  • Round 1: Quiz
    Questions are based on fun, logical, and technical along with some basic electrical questions. The contestant answering the maximum no. of questions within the time limit will be promoted to round 2.
  • Round 2: Error Detection
    A code containing errors will be shown on the screen. The competitor who identifies most of the mistakes in the allotted time will advance to the third round.
  • Round 3: Codewars
    The entire code is shown on the screen, and the participant must enter it without looking at the monitor. Individuals that have zero errors in their code will be considered
  • Note:
    In round 3 the monitor will be turned to the other side ,so that its not visible to the contestant .

Registration Fee:

Rs 50 for one person
For 2 members Rs 80/-

Winners

Winners will be selected on the basis of fairness in playing game

Whoever qualifies all the 3 rounds within the short period of time will be considered as the winner.
